So I hear you are wanting to feel more relaxed and calm. Whether it be exam stress or people-induced stress, I've got just the places in the beautiful city of Johannesburg for you.

Head over to the Four Seasons Spa in Westcliff where you'll be greeted with heartwarming service plus a massage that will have you feeling relaxed to the max! This facility also offers body masks, facials, mud baths, seaweed body wraps, and so much more!

Address: 67 Jan Smuts Ave, Westcliff, Johannesburg, 2132, South Africa

Head over to the Wits Art Museum for an opportunity to check out local artists and their work. You will gain a new perspective and also feel extremely calm while doing so. Also, free food is available!

Address: Wits Art Museum Cnr Jorissen and, Bertha St, Johannesburg, 2001, South Africa
