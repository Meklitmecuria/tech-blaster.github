So I hear you are wanting to energetic and adventurous. Well I guarentee, you'll be extremely hype and off the walls after paying these spot a visit!

Address: R556, Sun City, 0316, South Africa

Check out Gold Reef City Theme Park for an adventure park experience like never before! My favorite (& scariest) ride is the Anaconda! Head to the Casino if you're feeling up for it!

Address: Northern Pkwy & Data Cres, Johannesburg, 2159, South Africa
