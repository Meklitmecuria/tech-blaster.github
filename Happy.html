Food always makes me happy! Below are some of my restaurant recommendations:

Address: 83 Rivonia Rd, Sandhurst, Sandton, 2196, South Africa

Check out O'Galitos for divine Portuguese and South African cuisine. My personal recommendation is the Grilled Salmon.

Address: 1303 Heuwel Rd, Centurion Central, Centurion, 0157, South Africa
